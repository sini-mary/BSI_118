Welcome to The Mini Ultimatum Game

In this game, you will take on one of the three roles below:

Player 1: You have been endowed with Ksh 200 and must decide how much to send to Player 2.

Player 3 (The Punisher): You will observe the transaction, what Player 1 has decided to send Player 2. Depending on your judgment, you can choose to "Punish" or "Not Punish" Player 1.

Player 2: You will receive an amount from Player 1 depending on The Punisher's decision.

Remember, your decisions will affect your earnings, so choose wisely!
